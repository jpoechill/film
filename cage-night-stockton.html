Synopsis

Set over the course of one fight night, we follow multiple perspectives — a rookie Khmer fighter, his trainer, a rival, and a promoter — as tensions boil before a brutal main event. The film explores themes of honor, family, and the price of ambition in the underground fighting circuit of Stockton, California.
